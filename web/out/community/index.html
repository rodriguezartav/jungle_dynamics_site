About Jungle Dynamics

The work of Arnoldo Mora and Roberto Rodriguez; Currently in Planning and Funding Stages.

What do we do?

We are a Start Up investment firm, based on contemporary business dynamics of high end technology software development that support the creating of high tech start ups and communities in Latin America.

How do we do it

By helping VC's participate in the first startup stages, securing their angel investments; By promoting a community and recruiting best resources and serving as a catalyst to Start Ups during the first most delicate stages.
