Internet Startup Acceleration Model

Technical Mentoring

We created and Open Sourced Sixth Framework to jump start the development of prototypes and beta versions of mobile/web applications.

With active participation and promotion of the Open Source Community the recruiting and training process is a year round activity.

Which yields in the involvement of technical, business and PR staff needed for the success of a Tech Startup

Business Jumpstart

The projects first steps must be directed completely towards the materialization of business ideas.

During the three month acceleration process legal and operating requirements are met with the help of partners in law and electronic commerce.

But business is not only paperwork, during the same period every aspect of the business model and investment options are fine tuned in consulting rounds with industry experts.

Media & Marketing

The most important part of an App, be it Web, App Store, Android Store , Chrome Store, etc; Is the Public Relations behind it.

Knowing how to pitch to media and newspapers, keeping the hype up with initial customers and spreading applications virally are part of the social science of Public Relations.

During the three month period, our our PR Rock-Stars execute the launch plan, in conjunction with preparing the staff the will accompany start ups on the public release and further.

Investor Relations

The goal of our three month process is to create the perfect environment for young start ups, help them grow focused and strong into solid corporations that after several rounds of investment and several thousands registered users grow to become multi-million dollar corporations.

After that Jungle Dynamics funded start ups are prepared for their private round of investment on D-DAY, where an exclusive group of investors attend the projects launch presentations.

Finally a public round of investment is planned to greet smaller VC's and media; creating the hype needed for public release of Beta Versions.

Community

We are promoting the creation of the Open Source Community for Ruby and Javascript Developers. Such technologies are the latest , in highest demand and highest paying in London, New york and San Francisco.

During 2012 we start the provision of resources and networking to start the community with events, presentations and developer gatherings; Where leader local and international developers present their work and ideas to inspire community members and improve their abilities.

Knowledge

Along with the creating of the community, Jungle Dynamics is developing and supporting several frameworks under MIT Open Source Licensing.

By participating and speaking at conferences in Europe , North and South America during the past 8 years and collaborating in the Development and Improvement of Open Source frameworks; We are at a point, we we can share such connections , experience and knowledge with Communities in Costa Rica.

Training

Open Source Frameworks , Community Events and Documented Tutorials are hosted in our Web Site and in GitHub Open Source Social Repository of Code; Where this and any other community can benefit from introductory to advanced step by step tutorials on different database, back-end and front-end technologies.
